How It Works

Four steps to being REAL verified.

01

Verify your identity

You submit your government ID through Stripe Identity — the same enterprise-grade verification technology used by banks and global financial platforms. Biometric matching, liveness detection, document authentication. Your ID document is never stored by REAL. Stripe handles it entirely and simply confirms you are who you say you are.

02

Prove you control every account you own

For each platform you operate on, you add your unique REAL verification code to the first line of your bio. This code is generated by REAL and is specific to you. The act of placing it there proves you control that account — only the person holding the login credentials can edit a profile bio. A fake account cannot replicate this step. They do not control your real Instagram, your real TikTok, your real YouTube. They cannot place your code in bios they don't have access to. Every account where your code appears is confirmed as genuinely yours.

03

Receive your REAL ID and shield badge

You receive a unique REAL ID number, a permanent shield badge in all formats, and your public profile page. REAL IDs are sequential, unique and permanent — never reassigned, never duplicated, never reused. The first 100 founding members receive RL-000001 through RL-000100. Every member after continues in sequence from RL-000101 onwards. A low number cannot be purchased after the fact. Your verification is timestamped permanently.

04

Put your REAL link everywhere

The REAL profile link goes in every bio, every platform, every email signature. It is the single place anyone can check to confirm the real you. A link can only be placed in a bio by the person who controls that account — so every platform showing your REAL link is confirmed as genuinely yours.

Common Questions

The questions people ask.

"Does having a REAL profile make me more vulnerable to being hacked or impersonated?"

No. The REAL public profile only shows information you have already chosen to make public yourself. Your Instagram handle is already public. Your TikTok is already public. Your website is already public. REAL simply links them together under a verified umbrella. Your government ID, your address, your date of birth — none of that appears on your REAL profile. You control exactly what is shown.

"Can't someone just write a REAL ID number in their fake bio?"

Yes — they can write the number. But the number alone means nothing. The protection comes from what happens when anyone searches it. That search takes them directly to the genuine verified profile — which shows the real person's exact platform handles. If the fake account's handle doesn't match, it is immediately exposed. Anyone can write a number. Nobody can fake the verified record behind it.

"What if someone finds a way to fake a REAL verification?"

No verification system can promise it is unfakeable forever. What REAL honestly promises is this — faking it requires passing government ID verification with a forged document through Stripe Identity's biometric matching and liveness detection, which constitutes serious criminal fraud in the UK. The effort and risk versus the reward makes this practically impossible for the overwhelming majority of bad actors. And your verified timestamp predates any fraudulent copy — your history cannot be rewritten.

"What if a big platform builds something like this?"

Platforms have a financial incentive to keep fake accounts. Fake accounts generate traffic and engagement. They have no incentive to solve this properly across platforms. And if a big company does eventually build something similar — REAL's founding members have verified history that cannot be replicated overnight. Being early is the protection.
