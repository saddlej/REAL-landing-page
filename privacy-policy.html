Section 01

Who We Are

REAL (trading name, hereinafter "REAL", "we", "us", "our") operates the website realverified.co.uk. REAL is a cross-platform identity verification platform that enables individuals to prove ownership of their online accounts and social media profiles.

For the purposes of UK GDPR and EU GDPR, REAL is the data controller — meaning we are responsible for deciding how and why your personal data is processed.

Data Controller Contact All privacy enquiries should be sent to: info@realverified.co.uk
Postal address available on request.
Section 02

Data We Collect

We collect different categories of data at different stages of your relationship with REAL.

Waitlist Signups

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Social media handle (optional)
  • Platform of interest (optional)
  • Date and time of signup

Verified Members (when the service launches)

When you become a verified member, we will additionally collect:

  • Billing information (processed and stored by Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Social media handles and platform links you choose to verify
  • Verification timestamps for each linked account
  • Your unique REAL ID number

Identity Verification

Important — Government ID Government-issued identity documents and biometric data are processed exclusively by Stripe Identity, our verification partner. REAL never receives, stores, or has access to your actual ID document. Stripe confirms to us only that verification was successful. Stripe's own privacy policy governs the handling of that data.

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type

This data is used solely for security, performance monitoring, and improving the service. It is not used to identify you individually.

Section 04

Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, we are required to have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

Consent

Where you have actively agreed to receive communications from us — for example, by joining the waitlist and opting in to updates. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us or clicking unsubscribe in any email.

Contract Performance

Processing necessary to deliver the REAL service to you — including verification, billing, account management, and member communications.

Legitimate Interests

Processing that is in our legitimate business interests, provided it does not override your rights — including maintaining the security of our platform, fraud prevention, and improving the service using anonymised data.

Legal Obligation

Where we are required by law to process or retain certain data — for example, financial records under UK tax law.

Section 06

International Data Transfers

REAL is based in the United Kingdom. Our primary database (Supabase) is hosted in the EU West (Ireland) region, keeping your data within the European Economic Area.

Some of our third-party providers (including Stripe and Vercel) may process data in the United States and other countries. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the UK ICO or European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Providers' adherence to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where relevant

By using REAL from any country, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take all reasonable steps to ensure your data remains protected to the standards described in this policy.

Section 08

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. These rights apply regardless of where in the world you are based — we honour them globally.

👁

Right of Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

🗑

Right to Erasure

You can ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

⏸

Right to Restriction

You can ask us to pause processing your data in certain circumstances.

📦

Right to Portability

You can request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

🚫

Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.

🤖

Automated Decisions

You have rights regarding any automated decision-making that significantly affects you.

↩️

Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@realverified.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Right to Complain

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority:

  • UK: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk
  • EU: Your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)
  • Other countries: Your national data protection regulator

We would always prefer to resolve concerns directly — please contact us first and we will do our best to put things right.

Section 09

Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. Measures we have in place include:

  • All data stored in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled
  • Data encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS
  • Government ID documents processed solely by Stripe Identity — we never receive or store them
  • Payment card data processed solely by Stripe — we never receive or store card numbers
  • Access to data limited to authorised personnel only
  • Regular review of security practices as the platform scales

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay, as required by UK GDPR.

Section 11

Children

REAL is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at info@realverified.co.uk and we will delete it promptly.

Section 12

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights in relation to your personal information.

We do not sell personal information. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months and do not intend to do so.

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about them
  • Know whether their personal information is sold or disclosed, and to whom
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (not applicable — we do not sell data)
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of their personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

To exercise your California rights, contact us at info@realverified.co.uk.
