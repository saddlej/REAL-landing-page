Section 02

What REAL Is — and Is Not

REAL is an independent cross-platform identity verification platform. It enables individuals to verify their government-issued identity once, link their online accounts across multiple platforms, and receive a unique REAL ID number and public profile that demonstrates genuine ownership of those accounts.

What REAL provides

  • A verified public profile showing your confirmed handles across linked platforms
  • A unique, permanent REAL ID number
  • A shield badge you can display to signal your verified status
  • A trust timeline showing your verified history
  • A publicly searchable record that confirms the real you

What REAL does not provide

Important Limitation REAL is a proof layer and source of truth. It cannot prevent fake accounts from being created. It makes fake accounts immediately and permanently identifiable. REAL does not guarantee the removal of impersonating accounts from any platform. REAL does not provide legal services. REAL does not provide law enforcement services.

The value of REAL is that a fake account either has no REAL link — which exposes them — or copies your REAL link — which opens your real verified profile and exposes them. The mechanism works because only you can place a link in your own bio.
